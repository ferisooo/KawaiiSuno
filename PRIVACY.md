# Privacy Policy — Suno Kawaii Player

**Last updated: 2026-06-20**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Suno Kawaii Player** (the "app") handles your information.
The app is a free, open-source desktop program created by **Feris** (the "developer").

> **Short version:** The developer does **not** collect, receive, store, see, or sell any of your
> data. There is no account, no server, and no tracking built into this app. Everything the app
> creates stays on **your own computer**.

---

## 1. The developer collects nothing

Suno Kawaii Player has **no backend server, no analytics, no telemetry, and no user account** operated
by the developer. The developer (Feris) does not receive any information about you, your device, your
listening habits, or your usage of the app. There is nothing to collect, because the app never sends
your data to the developer.

## 2. What the app stores — and where

Everything the app saves is written **only to a folder on your own computer** (your operating system's
standard application-data location for this app). This may include:

- the list of Suno songs you imported and their metadata (title, cover image URL, etc.);
- your playlists, favorites, and app settings (volume, equalizer, etc.);
- optionally cached audio files, if you turn on the **Offline cache** feature;
- optionally a **DeepSeek API key**, if you choose to enter one in the "Create" tab;
- your **Suno login session** (cookies/tokens), stored locally so you don't have to sign in every time.

None of this leaves your computer except as needed to talk **directly** to the third-party services
listed below. You can delete all of it at any time by removing the app's data folder or uninstalling
the app.

## 3. Third-party services you may connect to

When you use certain features, the app connects **directly** from your computer to third-party
services. The developer is not a party to those connections and does not see that traffic. Each
service has its own privacy policy, which governs any data you send to it:

- **Suno** (`suno.com`, `cdn*.suno.ai`) — when you browse Suno, sign in, or import/download songs.
  Your Suno account and activity are between **you and Suno**, under Suno's privacy policy and terms.
- **GitHub** (`raw.githubusercontent.com`, `api.github.com`) — used only to check for and download app
  updates. These are ordinary web requests and do not include personal information you've entered.
- **DeepSeek** (`api.deepseek.com`) — used **only if** you open the "Create" tab and provide your own
  API key. The text prompts you submit are sent to DeepSeek under DeepSeek's privacy policy and terms.
- **Google Fonts** (`fonts.googleapis.com`, `fonts.gstatic.com`) — used to load display fonts. As with
  any website, your IP address may be visible to the font host as part of a normal request. The app
  includes system-font fallbacks so it still works offline.

The developer does not control these services and is not responsible for their data practices. Please
review their policies directly.

## 4. No selling or sharing of personal data

Because the developer collects no personal data, the developer does not — and cannot — sell, rent,
trade, or share your personal information with anyone.

## 5. Children

The app is not directed to children. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including
children, because it collects nothing.

## 6. Your control over your data

You are always in full control. You can:

- turn features (such as Offline cache or the "Create" tab) on or off at any time;
- clear cached audio from within the app's Settings;
- delete the app's data folder to erase all stored songs, playlists, settings, keys, and login session;
- uninstall the app to remove it entirely.

## 7. Security

Your data lives on your own device and is subject to your device's own security. The app is open
source, so anyone may inspect exactly what it does (see the README's "Is this a virus?" section).

## 8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published in this file in the project
repository, with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after a change means you
accept the revised policy.
